Mini Discovery Sessions: Support for Strategic Thinking in Digital Change

Are you navigating service transformation, but finding it hard to stop and think?

Our Mini Discovery Sessions offer a focused space to explore real-world challenges affecting staff and service users—so you can move from firefighting to forward planning.

What You’ll Get

  • Clarity on your challenge and its root causes
  • A light touch Theory of Change map to link activities with outcomes
  • Shared understanding across teams or services
  • Ideas for building internal capability, improving staff experience, or unlocking value from your existing tools (including ours)

Who it's for

  • Digital teams looking to support change across the organisation
  • Transformation leads working on culture, adoption or cross-team alignment
  • Service teams involved in redesign or automation efforts
  • Anyone exploring how to make digital work better for staff and citizens

Why Focus on Employee Experience?

Digital change isn’t just about tech—it’s about people. Many transformation efforts stall because:

  • Staff feel excluded, overwhelmed or bypassed
  • Capability gaps aren’t identified or addressed
  • New systems create unintended burdens

How It Works

  • Duration: 60–90 minutes (remote or in person)
  • Facilitator: A digital and service design specialist with experience in local government and SaaS transformation
  • Output: A summary of insights, a lightweight outcomes map, and optional recommendations
